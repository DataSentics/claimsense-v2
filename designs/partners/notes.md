# ClaimSense Partners page — design notes

> Route: `/partners/` — linked from the footer (Company).
> Status: `needs-functional-spec` | `approval_required: commission, revenue and market claims; tier obligations; form workflow`
> Primary conversion: **Submit Partner Application**

---

## Visual system
Consistent with the site: Tosh A + Roboto, Bull Orange `#FF5539` / Dream-in-Blue `#002870`. Dark navy hero (revenue/partnership = ambition), warm + white sections, secondary palette for the hero metrics and revenue figures. Header CTA reads "Become a Partner" (page context) instead of the global "Book a Demo".

## Layout decisions
- **Dark hero** — eyebrow "Partner Program", H1, intro, dual CTA (Become a Partner / View Partner Tiers), and the 4 hero metrics (50% / 10x / €400k+ / 25+) in a bordered strip.
- **Partner Tiers** — 4 cards (Registered / Silver / Gold "Most Popular" highlighted / Platinum), each with commission %, Requirements list, Benefits list (green ✓), and typical deals/year. All verbatim.
- **Partner Benefits** — 6 cards, verbatim.
- **Revenue illustration** — two panels: Services Revenue Breakdown (light) + "Business generated by average partner / In 12 months" (dark navy, with the €500k–€920k total highlighted).
- **Apply Now** — Partner application form, verbatim fields from `forms.md` (First/Last name, Work Email, Company, Website, Partner Type, Country, Tell us about your business), submit "Submit Partner Application".
- Footer "Partners" link wired to this page across all pages; Partners marked active here.

## Section order
Hero (+ metrics) → Partner Tiers → Partner Benefits → Revenue illustration → Apply Now → Footer. Content file order preserved.
