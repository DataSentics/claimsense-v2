One master claim · multiple workstreams

One claim. One shared operational view.

Complex claims often need legal, finance and operational specialists to contribute at the same time. ClaimSense keeps the customer outcome connected while each team works in its own linked workstream.

  • ●One master claim keeps the full customer context in one place
  • ●Specialist workstreams give legal, finance and assessment teams clear ownership
  • ●Shared documents and facts reduce duplicate requests and handovers
  • ●One end-to-end task owner sees what is missing without chasing every operational detail
