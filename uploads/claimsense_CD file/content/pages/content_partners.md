## Partner Tiers

Choose your partnership level. Progress through tiers as you grow. Higher tiers unlock better margins, more support, and exclusive opportunities.

### Registered
- 20% — Commission on licence fees
- **Requirements:** Complete partner onboarding; Pass product certification; Assign dedicated resource
- **Benefits:** Partner portal access; Sales enablement materials; Basic technical support; Lead registration
- **Typical deals/year:** 1-2

### Silver
- 35% — Commission on licence fees
- **Requirements:** All Registered requirements; 2+ certified consultants; Joint marketing commitment
- **Benefits:** Everything in Registered; Priority lead sharing; Co-marketing funds; Quarterly business reviews
- **Typical deals/year:** 3-5

### Gold — Most Popular
- 45% — Commission on licence fees
- **Requirements:** All Silver requirements; 5+ certified consultants; Dedicated partner manager; Case study participation
- **Benefits:** Everything in Silver; First-look at new features; Joint solution development; Executive sponsorship; Premium support SLA

### Platinum
- 50% — Commission on licence fees

- **Benefits:** Everything in Gold; Product roadmap influence; White-label options; Exclusive territory rights; Revenue guarantees; Executive advisory board
- **Typical deals/year:** 10+
