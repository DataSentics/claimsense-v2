## Common Questions

Quick answers to the questions we hear most from claims teams evaluating ClaimSense.

1. **What exactly does ClaimSense do?**  
   ClaimSense is an AI-native platform that runs the full claim lifecycle — from intake to resolution. AI reads all documents, extracts key data, creates a case summary, and proposes a decision with reasoning. Your handler reviews and decides in minutes instead of hours.
2. **Does AI make decisions for us?**  
   No. AI pre-processes the case, creates a summary, and proposes a decision with transparent reasoning. The final decision always stays with your handler. We monitor AI accuracy in real time — if it drops, the system flags it and you adjust the rules.
3. **Is it secure? Where does our data live?**  
   Your data stays in your environment. ClaimSense runs on your own cloud infrastructure — Azure, GCP, or AWS — via a Databricks-based deployment. Full audit trail, role-based access control, and enterprise-grade security.
4. **How much does it cost and how do we start?**  
   Pricing is per claim, not per licence. Analytical Agent starts at €3/claim, Full Operational Platform at €5/claim. Cloud infrastructure, AI tokens, updates, and security are included — no hidden fees.
