# ClaimSense Platform Features

Four pillars of an AI-native claims platform — from automated case handling to enterprise-grade infrastructure.

## 01 — Automated Case Pre-processing by AI Agents

Every incoming claim is picked up by AI agents that analyze, structure, and enrich the case before it reaches a human handler. The agents extract key information from all documents and communications, build a complete case summary with suggested outcomes, and log every step of their reasoning.

- Automatic document extraction & structuring
- AI-generated case summaries with suggested outcomes
- Full reasoning log & transparent decision trail
- Complete data historization & version tracking
- Built-in audit trail for every agent action

**Asset:** Claims dashboard with AI-proposed case statuses.

# 02 — Master Claims, Specialist Workstreams & Agentic Workflows

Complex claims rarely belong to one person or one department. ClaimSense connects
specialist workstreams to one master claim, so the customer context remains complete
while every team has a clear, focused responsibility.

- One master claim as the shared customer-centric record
- Linked specialist workstreams for legal, finance, assessment and other functions
- Shared documents, facts, correspondence and decision history
- One accountable end-to-end task with linked process subtasks
- Configurable approval chains, escalation rules and workflow versioning

**Visual:** Master claim hierarchy with specialist owners, shared case file and master-task
dependency view. Use `Illustrative workflow` unless an approved product screenshot is supplied.

## 03 — Chat Across All Cases & Interactive Claim Analytics

Ask questions across your entire claim portfolio in natural language. Instantly search documents, emails, call recordings, and notes — then drill into analytics dashboards that reveal patterns, bottlenecks, and satisfaction drivers. Management gets real-time interactive insights to steer operations.

- Natural language search across all case data
- Interactive dashboards with drill-down analytics
- Trend analysis by handler, segment, product & time
- AI monitoring — detect accuracy drift & trigger reviews

**Asset:** Analytics Data Assistant with natural language queries.

## 04 — Full Claim Core System

The platform acts as a full claim management core, securely storing claim data and all processing actions. Complete audit trails, activity logging, and role-based access control ensure compliance and transparency. Enterprise-grade security standards protect all data and system access.

- Enterprise-grade security & encryption
- Complete audit trails & activity logging
- Role-based access control (RBAC)
- Compliance-ready architecture
- Parent-child claim relationships with traceable state history
- Access-controlled sharing of common case information across linked workstreams

**Assets:** Full linear history with complete audit trail; document extraction and field matching records.

## Deploy on Your Infrastructure

ClaimSense runs on Databricks and is fully deployable on your existing cloud infrastructure. Leverage your current data platform investments and simplify integration.

- Databricks — Unified data & AI platform
- Azure — Microsoft Azure Cloud
- GCP — Google Cloud Platform
- AWS — Amazon Web Services
