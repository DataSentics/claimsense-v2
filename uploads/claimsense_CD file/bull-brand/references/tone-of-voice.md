# Bull Brand Story & Tone of Voice

## Elevator Pitch — Long Version

In the age of AI and Advanced Computing, it's time to shape a more responsible digital world, driven by sustainability, sovereignty and real-world outcomes.

Born in 1931, Bull is a global leader in high-performance computing, artificial intelligence and quantum innovation.

Built on an open, end-to-end, and trusted foundation, Bull designs, deploys and runs hardware and software, while providing strategic services that unlock enterprise value, accelerate scientific research and drive society forward.

Driven by world-class R&D, manufacturing capabilities and data science expertise, Bull enables nations and industries to fully control their AI and data, advancing progress for the benefit of the planet.

Together with clients, partners and experts, Bull powers the innovation of tomorrow through performance and sustainability.

## Tone of Voice Attributes

### Humble and purposeful
Rooted in sovereignty, sustainability, and performance, Bull advances with respect for others and awareness of its responsibility, strong in its convictions, yet humble in its expression.

### Visionary and collaborative
Forward-looking and inspiring audiences to build what's possible with sovereign AI mastery, Bull inspires institutions, governments, and industries to own and lead their own destiny.

### Empowering by sovereignty
Actively encourages control, independence, and autonomy. Bull is a catalyst, giving stakeholders the tools and confidence to shape their AI journey and drives breakthroughs for business and society.

### Trustworthy and open
Always grounded in competence and transparency, Bull is reliable, trustworthy, and open. Its solutions are built by and for those who value true performance and sovereignty.

### Rooted in a strong heritage
Bull is proud of its European heritage and its mission as a champion for positive global sustainable impact.

## Guiding Principles

- **Strong culture, pride and trust**: Foster a strong company culture promoting belonging through collaboration and shared values
- **Agility at global scale**: Operate with flexibility, prioritizing simplicity and process simplification
- **Innovation that unlocks creative solutions**: Encourage forward-thinking decision-making and creative problem-solving
- **Growth powered by technological legacy**: Honor the legacy while leveraging it as a foundation for growth
- **Responsible innovation for a sustainable future**: Committed to a better future through responsible innovation and reducing environmental impact
